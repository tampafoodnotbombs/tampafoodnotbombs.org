We're Tampa Food Not Bombs

Tampa FNB provides mutual aid survival programs assisting the houseless and hungry, and stands in opposition to the violence of war and poverty. Grassroots organization (NOT a non profit) - We are by the people, for the people We have learned that organizing our community allows us to change our material conditions and meet our needs. We take care of us Tampa Chapter over 12 years Anti: White supremacy Capitalism Settler-colonialism Neocolonialism Racism Patriarchy Xenophobia Ableism Homophobia Transphobia And all other oppressive systems … imperialism.

We are dedicated to serving our community. We share resources with our community. We engage in conversation with our community. We provide free laundry services to all. We practice zero waste by rescuing surplus food items from local restaurants and grocers. We gather to watch documentaries and engage in meaningful discussions We ensure accommodations are available for non-native English speakers and disabilities We mask to keep our community safe during the ongoing covid 19 pandemic. We mask during our shares, at indoor gatherings, and agree to stay home if we are sick. We are not affiliated with any religion or religious entity. We are committed to centering BIPOC perspectives. We follow through not only with words, but with our actions. We are anti-carceral. We do not throw people away. We treat everyone with respect.
